Lemon Lime Bitters

Description

Quench your thirst with a glowing glass of this soft and soothing mocktail

Ingredients

  • 1.25 litres lemonade, chilled
  • 1/2 cup lime juice cordial
  • 1 tsp Angostura Bitters
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • Lemon slices, to serve

Instructions

  1. Combine lemonade, cordial and bitters in a jug.
  2. Divide ice between 4 glasses.
  3. Shake a few drops of bitters into each glass.
  4. Top with lemonade mixture.
  5. Serve with lemon slices.
